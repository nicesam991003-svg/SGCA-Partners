Management System Certification

In-depth expertise in foundational and specialized international standards.

ISO 9001 (Quality Management)

"The essential benchmark for quality demanded by the global market."

Overview

ISO 9001 is the international standard for quality management systems applicable to any industry. Beyond mere product quality, it certifies that an organization has established systematic processes to consistently meet customer requirements and drive continuous improvement.

Key Benefits

  • Building Customer Trust Enhance brand value by demonstrating quality management capabilities that align with international standards.
  • Operational Efficiency Reduce costs by eliminating redundant tasks and optimizing core business processes.
  • Competitive Edge in Tenders Meet mandatory requirements and secure advantage points in public and private procurement processes.

ISO 14001 (Environmental Management)

"A strategic necessity for sustainable growth and the cornerstone of ESG management."

Overview

This standard defines the management framework required to minimize the environmental impact of an organization's activities, products, and services. It is a management strategy that encompasses resource efficiency and regulatory compliance beyond basic environmental protection.

Key Benefits

  • Proactive Regulatory Response Manage risks associated with increasingly complex global environmental laws and Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanisms (CBAM).
  • Resource & Energy Optimization Achieve tangible reductions in operating costs through waste minimization and improved energy efficiency.
  • Eco-friendly Brand Image Secure external credibility by meeting demands for carbon neutrality and ESG information disclosure.

ISO 45001 (Occupational Health & Safety)

"The safety of your workforce is your company's greatest asset."

Overview

The international standard for preventing occupational injuries by identifying and managing workplace hazards. Its purpose is to create a safe work environment by continuously improving an organization's health and safety performance.

Key Benefits

  • Legal Risk Mitigation Demonstrate compliance with strengthened safety and health regulations, such as the Serious Accidents Punishment Act.
  • Reduced Accident Rates Prevent human and material losses and maintain productivity through systematic risk assessments.
  • Trusted Corporate Culture Attract top talent and improve labor relations by fostering a culture that prioritizes safety.

ISO 13485 (Medical Device Quality)

"The mandatory gateway for entering the global medical device market."

Overview

A quality management standard specifically tailored for the medical device industry. It evaluates the ability to ensure safety and effectiveness throughout the entire product lifecycle, from design and development to production, installation, and associated services.

Key Benefits

  • Global Regulatory Compliance Establish a core foundation for compliance with EU MDR/IVDR and US FDA regulations.
  • Guaranteed Quality Consistency Reduce product recall risks through rigorous risk management and validation processes.
  • Global Partnership Expansion Meet the minimum qualification requirements demanded by international buyers and distributors.

ISO 19443 (Nuclear Industry Quality)

"Committed to the world's highest standards of nuclear safety and quality."

Overview

Based on ISO 9001, this standard adds specific nuclear industry requirements to strengthen safety and quality throughout the supply chain. It focuses on IT security, supply chain management, and the cultivation of a safety culture.

Key Benefits

  • Global Nuclear Market Leadership Meet the international quality certification requirements essential for exporting nuclear power technology.
  • Supply Chain Integrity Prove high reliability, including the prevention of counterfeit or fraudulent items (CFSI) and IT security for critical components.
  • Safety Culture Integration Successfully embed the 'Safety First' principle, unique to the nuclear industry, across the entire organization.
